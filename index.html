As you step further into the BR Area, you’ll encounter an old kedai runcit (convenience store). Does it bring back any childhood memories? This decoration concept aims to evoke nostalgia, creating a space where colleagues can find joy and warmth in the office. It also reflects Senheng’s rapid expansion in the 2000s, when the company introduced the chain store concept, implemented a fixed price policy, and launched a customer loyalty program—key steps in becoming a trusted household name. The space symbolizes accessibility and Senheng’s growing presence across Malaysia, while evoking happy memories of local convenience stores from our childhood.
